Pulau Rawa

"Nothing soothes the soul like a day by the ocean!"

Pulau Rawa, Johor

About

Rawa Island is rich in eco-tourism activities and natural wonders for travellers to enjoy. Rawa Island is surrounded by crystal clear waters and gorgeous beaches. If you are a snorkelling enthusiast or you love diving, then you won't regret visiting this island.

Contact

+607-799 1204

Opening Hours

8:30am - 5pm

Address

Rawa Island, 86800 Mersing, Johor

Price/Entrance Fees

No permit fee; however course fees varies based on the activities.

What can we do?

Beach Volleyball

Need something else to do other than lazing on the beach or getting into the water? What better way to bond with your travel mates and maybe make new friends, than over a rousing game of beach volleyball by the ocean!

Snorkelling

Treat yourself to an amazing snorkelling experience surrounded by a wonderful variety of fish and marine species. You can choose to have guided snorkelling sessions if you are not familiar with the activity.

Water Sliding

The slides may belong to one of the resorts but nobody will try to stop you from sliding! Both slides will have you splashing into the sea with glee! The orange slide consists of some turns while the blue slide goes straight into the water!

Nearby food

Kluang Rail Cofee


Kluang Rail Coffee is synonymous with a quaint little town in Johor. Having been in operation since 78 years ago, the irresistible aroma of this kopitiam's coffee still lures loyal customers, the most famous among them being the Johor ruler Sultan Ibrahim Sultan Iskandar.


Opening Hours:

7am - 6pm

Mersing Seafood Restaurant


Mersing Seafood Restaurant is the destination you must go for fresh and delicious seafood. The air-conditioned restaurant, which clean and tidy decoration and furniture, is always crowded with customers especially during dinner hours.


Opening Hours:

12:30pm - 8pm

History 1948 Cafe & Bistro


Best place to hang out and have a beer and enjoy the evening in Mersing. Fussion food, like pizza was pretty good, despite the fact they used a tortilla for the crust. Great ambiance for this town.


Opening Hours:

5pm - 1am
